Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 27, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Wolfpack Daily Tracker collects only the data necessary to deliver a personalized elite performance experience. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Wolfpack Daily Tracker:

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you use Sign in with Apple, your Apple-provided identifier. You may choose to use Apple's "Hide My Email" relay — we support this fully.

Performance & Health Data

Data you enter into the app including daily goals (your 3 wins), tasks, hydration logs, workout completions, streak counts, and nutrition entries. This data is yours and is stored securely in your personal cloud account.

Progress Photos

If you choose to use the Progress Photos feature, images are uploaded to your private cloud storage. Photos are not accessible to anyone other than you.

Device & Usage Data

Basic technical information such as app version, iOS version, and crash reports — used solely to improve app stability. No advertising identifiers are collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your daily tracker experience
  • To sync your data across devices you own
  • To send you hydration and progress photo reminders (only if you grant notification permission)
  • To calculate your daily performance score and streaks
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the app's core functionality.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), which is secured with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Firebase is a Google service subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Progress photos are stored in Firebase Storage in a private bucket accessible only to your authenticated account. No public URLs are generated.

4. Third-Party Services

Wolfpack uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage
  • Apple Sign In — optional authentication method

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app via Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

Wolfpack Daily Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ngutierrez0120@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
