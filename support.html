Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Is my data backed up? +

Yes. All your data — goals, workouts, hydration logs, and progress photos — is synced securely to your account via Firebase. You won't lose anything if you switch phones or reinstall the app.

How do I enable hydration notifications? +

Go to the Schedule tab and tap the bell icon next to the Hydration section. Make sure notifications are enabled for Wolfpack in your iPhone Settings → Notifications.

Can I use the app on multiple devices? +

Yes. Log in with the same email and password on any device. Your data will sync automatically across all devices linked to your account.

How do I delete my account and data? +

You can request account deletion by emailing us at ngutierrez0120@gmail.com. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Why isn't the app saving my progress? +

Make sure you're connected to the internet when first logging in. If the problem persists, try logging out and back in. If it still doesn't work, contact us and we'll help you resolve it.

Is Wolfpack free? +

The core app is free to download and use. Premium features may be added in future updates — existing users will be notified before any paid features are introduced.
